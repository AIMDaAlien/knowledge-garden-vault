Until now, you've been limited to presentation and appearance of the content you create. To start taking control, add a `style` element within the `head` element.

```css
  <head>

    <style>

    <meta charset="utf-8" />

    <title>Cafe Menu</title>

    </style>

  </head>

  <body>

    <main>

      <h1>CAMPER CAFE</h1>

      <p>Est. 2020</p>

      <section>

        <h2>Coffee</h2>

      </section>

    </main>

  </body>
```
